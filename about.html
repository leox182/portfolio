About Me

Leonardo Mogollon

Puebla, Mexico

I am a web developer and designer originally from the city of San Felipe in the central west of Venezuela and currently reside in Puebla, Mexico. I am a very creative and passionate person who likes to learn new things. I have more than 4 years of experience in the industry. I am a critical thinker and I always look for the most objective information to help me make the best decisions for my projects.

Currently, I'm the Marketing Manager at Grupo Malfre, since May 2018. Grupo Malfre is a company that sells products and services for the home. Also, I've been a freelancer since the beginning of 2020.

Previously, I worked with my partners at Hermanos Mogollon, a company that operates bars and restaurants in Venezuela. My main function was to lead the creative team of the different establishments under the consortium to develop marketing strategies, web page development and establish our online presence. During my 3 years of work I helped directly in the growth of the company by opening 4 new establishments.
